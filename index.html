About The Atelier

Where refined craftsmanship meets the emotion of the moment.

RJ.DUNNI ATELIER creates dresses that feel deeply personal and unmistakably elevated. Every silhouette is shaped with precision, every finish is considered, and every detail is designed to make a woman feel luminous the moment she steps into the room.

From bridal statements to custom asoebi looks and luxury evening pieces, the experience is intimate, thoughtful and beautifully tailored from first sketch to final fitting.
